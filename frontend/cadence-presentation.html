The Problem

⏱️

Time-Intensive

Content teams spend 60%+ of their time writing, editing, and reformatting posts for different platforms, leaving no room for strategy.

🔀

Fragmented Tooling

Managing multiple websites across different social platforms means juggling 5+ tools — writers, schedulers, CMS, analytics — with no single source of truth.

🎨

Brand Inconsistency

Without a style-aware AI, generated content drifts from the brand voice, requires heavy editing, and still misses the tone that resonates with the audience.

💡  Our Solution

Cadence — One Platform, Every Channel

  • 🤖
    AI Content GenerationGPT-4o-mini generates brand-aware blogs, social captions, and scripts tailored to each website's tone and style guide.
  • 🕷️
    Intelligent Website CrawlerAutomatically crawls & analyses your website to extract brand voice, colour palette, topics, and tone — zero manual setup.
  • ✅
    Approval WorkflowAdmins review, approve, reject or regenerate AI drafts before anything goes live. Full editorial control without the overhead.
  • 🗓️
    Automated SchedulerCelery Beat fires every minute, picks up approved posts, and publishes them to the right channel at exactly the right time.

AI Content Pipeline

🕷️

Website Intelligence

Crawler extracts brand voice, tone, colors, key phrases, headings, and sentiment from the live site — building a rich style guide used in every generation prompt.

📝

Context-Aware Prompts

Each AI generation bundles the website style guide, sample content, target platform, meta-tags, and admin brief into a precision-crafted prompt for GPT-4o-mini.

🔄

Regeneration Support

Admins can reject and regenerate a draft. Celery re-runs the generation task using the original idea, deletes the old draft, and presents a fresh alternative.

🚀

Multi-Channel Publishing

Blogs go through the custom website API endpoint; social posts fire through make.com webhooks to Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, and YouTube simultaneously.

Roles, Permissions & Security

👤

Admin

Manages their own websites, submits content ideas, approves/rejects drafts, views analytics, and configures platform connections for their assigned sites.

👑

Super Admin

Full platform access — manages all websites across all admins, configures roles & permissions, accesses billing, and sees platform-wide analytics.

🔐

Security Highlights

  • ✓ JWT token authentication on all API routes
  • ✓ Role-based object permissions (DRF)
  • ✓ Social auth credentials stored encrypted
  • ✓ Soft-delete for drafts and websites
  • ✓ Activity log for all key actions
  • ✓ CORS & CSRF protection via Django
